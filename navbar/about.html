Meet the Owner & Our Production Process

Mike Shields, founder of Iron Valley Mead & Wine

Our mead is crafted through a meticulous process that honors traditional methods:

  1. Honey Selection: We source only the finest local honey, ensuring purity and natural flavor.
  2. Fermentation: Carefully monitored fermentation transforms the honey into a smooth, balanced mead.
  3. Aging: Our meads mature through glass jar fermentation to maintain clean and quality mead.
  4. Bottling & Quality Control: Every batch undergoes strict quality checks before bottling to guarantee excellence.
